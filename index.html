Team's histroy

19971 started in 2021 during the time of covid. It was an idea of a senoir named Hudson that wanted to leave a mark in Emerson histroy and create something that would last after he leaves. He gathered members from freshman to senoirs and gathered the information to form the team with help of our teacher, Mr.Dicker. The rookie team was formed of six core members. Through effort, struggle, and time the team was able to enter the state touraments in its first year.
